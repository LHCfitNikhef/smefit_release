Reproducing results

The runcards that are needed to reproduce the results can be found on the repo through this link. We provide runcards split into the following categories:

  • fits: contains runcards to launch fits. This is further subdivided into smefit_real, smefit_projections, and smefit_uv for fits to real data, projected data and UV complete models.

  • projections: contains runcards to produce projected datasets.

  • reports: contains runcards to produce fit reports
